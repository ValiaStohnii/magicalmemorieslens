These milestones underscore Magic Memory Lens' commitment to innovation, customer satisfaction, and community involvement throughout its 12-year journey.

  • 1. The Beginning

    Founded by Jane and Mark Reynolds, the studio started in a small storefront with a focus on quality and professionalism.

  • 2. Service Expansion

    Magic Memory Lens expanded services to include event photography, catering to weddings, graduations, and corporate events.

  • 3. Digital Transition

    Embraced digital technology, upgrading equipment for improved efficiency and higher-quality images.

  • 4. Community Engagement

    Engaged in community initiatives and charitable events, strengthening ties with clients and garnering recognition.

  • 5. Online Presence

    Launched an online platform for showcasing portfolios, interacting with clients, and introducing branded merchandise, expanding reach and brand presence.
